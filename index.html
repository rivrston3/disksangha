What is this

A frisbee.
A covenant.
A commons.

The frisbee has one rule: pass it on. So does this.

Alcente is a circle of friends who take friendship seriously — who believe that the art of genuine belonging, joyful play, and honest presence is one of the most important practices a person can undertake.

We collect and share the forms that make real connection possible: games, songs, rituals, conversations, and practices — at every scale from two people to a whole gathering. A living library, tended together.

  • The Library

    Open to anyone. A commons of practices for joyful, authentic, whole-hearted relating — searchable by scale and theme.

  • The Circle

    For those who take the vow. A hundred years of friendship. Monthly dispatches. Invitations. A crew committed to the long game.

  • The Disk

    The artifact that carries the covenant — from hand to hand, stranger to friend, field to festival. The vow travels with it.

What we practice

The arts of belonging

Joy. Trust. Intimacy. The capacity to truly see and be seen. These are not soft skills. They are the most urgent work of our time — and they can be practiced, taught, and passed on.
