the vow

a covenant carried by the disk — from hand to hand, stranger to friend, year to year.

I catch. I pass. I show up. I practice friendship as if it matters. I share what I know. I learn from what I don't. I take joy seriously. I don't take myself too seriously. I stay in it for a hundred years.

What the circle means, practically

  • i. A monthly dispatch. Stories, practices, and invitations from inside the circle — what people are doing, learning, making, playing with. Something worth reading.
  • ii. Access to add to the library. Circle members can submit practices, games, songs, stories, and rituals to the commons. Your knowledge belongs here.
  • iii. Seasonal invitations. Gatherings, calls, convergences. Occasional experiments. The circle meets when there's something worth meeting for.
  • iv. The long game. This is not a platform. There is no algorithm. It is a small, slow, intentional circle — for people who mean it.
